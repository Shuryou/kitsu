How to Have Class

Weaving

What's weaving? Well let's start with basics. You have 2 kinds of actions. Spells/Skill and Abilities. What is the difference you ask? Good question. Spells and Skills activate the Global Cooldown (GCD). Abilities don't, they have their own cooldown(CD). So what does this mean?
Simple, it means when you activate a spell/skill, you can immediately activate an ability following that. This is the idea of weaving. And it's important if you want to "optimize" your damage in any class.
In the rotations you will see -> or -Weave-> . These respectively show the order, but the -Weave-> means you should weave the next action between GCDs.
So pay attention to your class and learn what your Skills/Spells are, and what your abilities are.
