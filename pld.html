The Way of the Knight

My Rotation

So Imma level with you, I have an "ideal rotation" I use, not sure if it's best or not. But Here it is.

Single Target

Opener/Melee Rotation
Fast Blade -> Riot Blade -> Fight or Flight (FoF) -> Circle of Skorn (CoS) -> Goring Blade -> Spirits Within -> Fast Blade -> Riot Blade -> Royal Authority -> Atonement x3 -> Repeat until FoF is done -> Casting Rotation

**Note CoS and Spirits Within should be used on Cooldown (CD)**

Casting Rotation
Requiescat -> Holy Spirit x4 -> Confiteor -> Melee Rotation

Area of Effect

Melee Rotation
FoF -> CoS -> Total Eclipse -> Prominence -> Repeat until FoF ends
Casting Rotation
Requiescat -> Holy Circle x4 -> Confiteor -> Melee Rotation

Things to Note

Do not use Requiescat if FoF is active.
Do not use FoF if Requiescat is active. These buffs do NOT stack damage buffs.
Do not start a caster rotation unless
  1) You have full MP
  2) Requiescat is off CD

The Theory

So the theory here is that you cast Requiescat and it swaps your bar to a "Caster" bar. And at the end of the rotation swaps back to a "melee" bar.

Step 1: The Setup

For starters. I don't use many macros for Paladin. And it takes a bit of setup to do what I do with it. I do a trick similar to my Macro Menu.
So first I use hotbars 6 and 7 to set up my "hotbar 1."

Step2: The Macros

Start Requiescat Phase:
  /micon "Requiescat"
  /hotbar copy PLD 7 PLD 1
  /ac "Requiescat"
End Requiescat Phase:
  /micon "Confiteor"
  /hotbar copy PLD 6 PLD 1
  /ac "Confiteor"

Notes on my Macro System

First and foremost (cause I forget and do this frequently) Do Not use Confiteor until Global Cooldown (GCD) is done. If you do, your bar will switch back to Melee Rotation bar without casting Confiteor.
