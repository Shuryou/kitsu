Tips and Tricks

Useful Abbreviations

GCD Global Cooldown
HQ High Quality usually provides better stats on HQ gear

Stats Explained

Combat Classes
HP Health. No HP. No Life.
MP Mana. No Mana. No Magic.
Strength Main stat to increase Physical Damage for Melee classes.
Dexterity Main stat to increase Physical damage for Ranged Classes and Ninja
Vitality Directly afffects maximum HP
Intelligence Increases Magic Damage for Tanks and Caster DPS
Mind Increases Healing Potency & Magic Damage for Healers
Critical Hit Criticals means you deal above average damage, or restore above average health
Direct Hit Similar to critical, but not as drastic a change in the damage/heal
Determination Increases the base amount of damage/healing done
Skill Speed Decreases GCD of Skills (physical abilities) time between auto attacks
Spell Speed Decreases Cast time and Spell GCD
Tenacity Reduces damage taken as a tank, as well as how much HP is restored by spells
Piety Increases MP regen for Healers ONLY
Crafters
Craftmanship Higher craftmanship means more progress on synthesis actions
Control Higher control means more progress on Quality actions
CP More CP, more crafting abilities
Gatherers
Gathering Increases your chance to successfully gather an item.
Perception Increases the HQ% chance
GP More GP, more gathering actions
